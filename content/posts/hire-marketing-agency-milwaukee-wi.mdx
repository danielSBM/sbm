## How to Evaluate Marketing Agency Performance in Milwaukee

Measuring marketing agency performance requires focusing on revenue-generating metrics rather than activity-based reporting. Milwaukee businesses should establish clear performance benchmarks during agency selection and maintain consistent measurement throughout partnerships.

### Primary Performance Indicators

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Total marketing investment divided by new customers acquired. Milwaukee B2B companies average $340 CAC while B2C businesses average $127 CAC according to Wisconsin Marketing Association data.

**Return on Advertising Spend (ROAS)**: Revenue generated per dollar invested in marketing campaigns. Effective agencies deliver 4:1 ROAS minimum, with top performers achieving 8:1 or higher ratios.

**Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Total revenue expected from each acquired customer. Milwaukee service businesses average $3,400 CLV while product companies average $1,850 CLV over 24-month periods.

**Conversion Rate Optimization**: Percentage of prospects converting to customers through agency campaigns. Milwaukee agencies achieving 3.5%+ conversion rates demonstrate superior targeting and messaging capabilities.

### Secondary Metrics Worth Tracking

- Lead quality scores based on sales team qualification
- Time from lead generation to customer conversion
- Campaign-specific ROI by channel and geographic area
- Incremental revenue growth attributed to marketing initiatives

Demand weekly performance reports including raw data access through platforms like Google Analytics, Facebook Ads Manager, and CRM systems. Quality agencies provide dashboard access allowing real-time campaign monitoring without waiting for scheduled reports.

Monthly strategy reviews should include performance analysis, optimization recommendations, and forward-looking campaign adjustments based on market conditions affecting Milwaukee businesses.
