## How to evaluate digital marketing ROI in New York?

Digital marketing ROI in New York should be calculated using customer lifetime value minus total acquisition costs, not just immediate revenue. New York businesses averaging $5,000+ customer lifetime values should target 3:1-5:1 ROI ratios, while lower-value businesses need 5:1-10:1 ratios to justify marketing investments.

**Essential ROI Calculation Components:**

**Customer Lifetime Value (CLV):**
- Average purchase amount × Purchase frequency × Customer lifespan
- Include upsells, referrals, and repeat business
- Account for seasonal variations in New York market
- Factor in profit margins, not just revenue

**Total Acquisition Cost (TAC):**
- Agency fees + Ad spend + Internal time costs
- Include setup fees and platform costs
- Account for sales team time in conversion process
- Factor in opportunity costs of choosing one agency over another

**ROI Formula:**
ROI = (CLV - TAC) ÷ TAC × 100

**Example ROI Calculation:**
Manhattan law firm:
- Customer lifetime value: $25,000 average
- Monthly marketing investment: $8,000
- Customers acquired monthly: 4
- Total acquisition cost per customer: $2,000
- ROI: ($25,000 - $2,000) ÷ $2,000 × 100 = 1,150%
