## Measuring Google Ads Success for Atlanta Businesses

**Atlanta businesses should track customer lifetime value, cost per acquisition, and return on ad spend rather than vanity metrics like impressions or clicks to determine Google Ads campaign profitability.** Meaningful measurement focuses on business outcomes rather than advertising statistics.

### Key Performance Indicators That Matter

**Revenue-focused metrics** provide clear campaign evaluation:
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Total ad spend divided by new customers acquired
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: Revenue generated divided by advertising investment
- **Customer Lifetime Value to CAC Ratio**: Long-term customer value compared to acquisition cost
- **Lead-to-customer conversion rate**: Percentage of leads becoming paying customers

**Atlanta-specific benchmarks** for campaign evaluation:
- **Acceptable CAC**: 15-25% of customer lifetime value
- **Target ROAS**: 3:1 minimum, 5:1 optimal
- **Lead quality score**: Based on geographic relevance, budget qualification, and timeline
- **Speed to conversion**: Time from click to customer (Atlanta average: 7-14 days)
