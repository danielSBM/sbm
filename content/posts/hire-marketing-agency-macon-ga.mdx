## How to Evaluate Marketing Agency Results in Macon

Measuring marketing performance requires focusing on business-impacting metrics rather than superficial engagement statistics that don't translate to revenue growth.

### Primary Performance Indicators
**Customer Acquisition Rate:** Track new customers generated monthly from marketing efforts. Effective campaigns should show consistent month-over-month growth.

**Cost Per Customer:** Calculate total marketing investment divided by new customers acquired. This metric directly impacts profitability and budget allocation decisions.

**Customer Lifetime Value Ratio:** Compare marketing costs to long-term customer value. Ratios above 3:1 indicate profitable marketing investments.

**Revenue Attribution:** Measure actual revenue generated from marketing channels, not just leads or inquiries. Many Macon businesses discover that high lead volumes don't correlate with sales growth.
