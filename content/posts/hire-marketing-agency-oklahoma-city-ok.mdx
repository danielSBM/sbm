## How Do You Evaluate Marketing Agency Performance?

Marketing agency performance measurement requires establishing clear key performance indicators (KPIs) before campaign launch. Oklahoma City businesses should demand monthly reporting on metrics directly tied to revenue generation rather than vanity metrics like impressions or followers.

**Primary Performance Indicators:**

1. **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Total marketing spend divided by new customers acquired
2. **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: Revenue generated per dollar invested in advertising
3. **Lead Quality Score**: Percentage of leads that convert to paying customers  
4. **Market Share Growth**: Increased visibility for target keywords in Oklahoma City searches
5. **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Long-term revenue potential of acquired customers

Traditional agencies often focus on activity metrics—emails sent, posts published, meetings held—rather than business outcomes. This approach obscures actual performance and makes ROI calculation impossible.

Effective agencies provide transparent reporting with clear causation between marketing activities and business results. They should demonstrate how specific campaigns drive phone calls, website inquiries, and in-store visits for Oklahoma City locations.

**Red Flags in Agency Reporting:**
- Emphasis on impressions rather than conversions
- Vague language about "brand awareness" without measurement
- Resistance to sharing advertising account access
- Monthly reports without actionable recommendations
- Inability to calculate customer acquisition costs
