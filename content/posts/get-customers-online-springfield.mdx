## Common Springfield Online Marketing Mistakes to Avoid

**Geographic Targeting Errors**: Many Springfield businesses make the mistake of targeting too broadly (entire state of Missouri) or too narrowly (city limits only). Optimal targeting includes Springfield plus surrounding communities within 30-35 miles.

**Ignoring Mobile Optimization**: 67% of Springfield local searches happen on mobile devices. Websites that aren't mobile-responsive lose up to 40% of potential customers before they engage.

**Inconsistent Business Information**: Different phone numbers, addresses, or business names across online directories confuse search engines and customers. This inconsistency can drop local search rankings by 50% or more.

**Review Management Neglect**: Springfield consumers read an average of 7 reviews before contacting local businesses. Companies that don't actively manage and respond to reviews lose customers to competitors with stronger online reputations.

**Single-Channel Focus**: Relying exclusively on one marketing channel (SEO-only or Facebook-only) creates vulnerability when algorithm changes or increased competition affect performance. Successful Springfield businesses diversify across 3-4 complementary channels.
