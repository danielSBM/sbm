## Measuring Facebook Ad Success: Provo Business Metrics

Facebook advertising success requires tracking specific metrics aligned with business growth objectives rather than vanity metrics like reach, impressions, or social media followers that provide no measurable ROI. ### Essential Performance Metrics

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**
Total advertising spend divided by new customers acquired provides the fundamental metric for Facebook advertising ROI. Successful Provo campaigns typically achieve CAC between $25-$150 depending on industry and average transaction value.

**Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**
Revenue generated divided by advertising costs measures immediate campaign profitability. Sustainable Provo campaigns maintain ROAS above 3:1 ($3 revenue per $1 advertising spend) with top performers achieving 5:1 or higher ratios.

**Conversion Rate by Traffic Source**
Facebook traffic conversion rates should exceed website average by 15-25% due to targeted audience quality. Lower conversion rates indicate targeting or landing page optimization problems requiring immediate attention.

**Cost Per Lead/Conversion**
Industry-specific benchmarks guide budget allocation decisions. Provo professional services average $25-$65 per qualified lead, while retail businesses target $8-$25 per online purchase or store visit.
