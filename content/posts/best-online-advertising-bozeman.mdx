## How to Measure Online Advertising Success in Bozeman

Successful online advertising measurement for Bozeman small businesses requires tracking customer acquisition cost (CAC), lifetime value (LTV), and return on ad spend (ROAS) monthly, with CAC under $75 and ROAS above 3:1 indicating healthy performance.

### Key Performance Indicators That Matter

Most advertising agencies focus on vanity metrics like impressions, clicks, and reach. These metrics feel good but don't correlate with business growth. Smart Bozeman businesses track metrics that directly impact profitability.

**Primary Metrics:**
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Total advertising spend divided by new customers acquired
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS):** Revenue generated divided by advertising spend
- **Lifetime Value (LTV):** Average total revenue per customer over their relationship
- **Conversion Rate:** Percentage of ad clicks that become customers

**Secondary Metrics:**
- **Cost Per Click (CPC):** Early indicator of campaign efficiency
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Measures ad relevance and appeal
- **Local Search Ranking:** Position for key Bozeman-specific searches
- **Customer Retention Rate:** Percentage of customers who return
