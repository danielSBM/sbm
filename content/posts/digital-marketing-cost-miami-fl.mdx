## What questions should Miami businesses ask marketing agencies?

**Miami businesses should prioritize agencies that guarantee measurable results, understand local market dynamics, and offer flexible pricing models aligned with business outcomes rather than input metrics.** The right questions reveal whether agencies deliver genuine value or sophisticated sales presentations.

### Essential Performance and Accountability Questions

**"What specific business results do you guarantee?"**
Effective agencies commit to customer acquisition numbers, lead generation targets, or revenue outcomes. Agencies discussing impressions, reach, or engagement without connecting to business growth lack confidence in their results.

**"How do you handle underperformance?"**
Quality agencies offer refunds, additional work, or campaign pivots when missing targets. Agencies blaming market conditions, competition, or client factors for poor results rarely improve performance.

**"What's your average client retention rate?"**

**"What Miami industries do you specialize in?"**

**"What are the total costs, including all fees and markups?"**
Request comprehensive pricing breakdowns including setup fees, platform markups, creative costs, and management fees. Hidden costs often double initial pricing estimates.
