## Measuring Success: Key Performance Indicators for Cedar Rapids Online Advertising

Effective online advertising measurement for Cedar Rapids small businesses requires focusing on revenue-generating metrics rather than vanity statistics like impressions, likes, or website traffic that don't correlate with business growth.

**Primary KPIs for Cedar Rapids Business Advertising:**

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Calculation:** Total advertising spend ÷ number of new customers acquired
- **Cedar Rapids benchmarks:** $85-250 depending on industry
- **Optimization target:** Decreasing CAC over time as campaigns mature

### Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- **Calculation:** Average purchase value × purchase frequency × customer lifespan
- **Cedar Rapids advantage:** Higher retention rates due to limited local competition
- **Target ratio:** CLV should exceed CAC by 3:1 minimum

### Return on Advertising Spend (ROAS)
- **Calculation:** Revenue generated ÷ advertising spend
- **Industry benchmarks for Cedar Rapids:**
  - Home services: 4:1 to 8:1
  - Professional services: 5:1 to 10:1
  - Retail/E-commerce: 2:1 to 4:1
  - Healthcare: 6:1 to 12:1

- **Qualification criteria:** Budget, timeline, decision-making authority

- **Intent level:** Research phase vs ready-to-buy signals
