## How Can Bozeman Businesses Measure Online Marketing Success?

**Effective measurement for Bozeman businesses focuses on customer acquisition cost, lifetime value, and return on ad spend rather than vanity metrics like website traffic or social media followers.** Small businesses need every marketing dollar to generate measurable revenue impact.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) That Matter

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Total marketing spend divided by new customers acquired. Bozeman businesses should target CAC below 30% of average customer value.

**Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Average revenue per customer over their entire relationship. This metric determines maximum sustainable acquisition costs.

**Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: Revenue generated divided by advertising investment. Profitable Bozeman businesses typically achieve 3:1 to 5:1 ROAS across all channels.

**Local Market Share**: Percentage of local searches, reviews, and social mentions compared to competitors. This matters more in Bozeman than absolute traffic numbers.
