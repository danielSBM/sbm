## Measuring Success: KPIs That Actually Matter

Most Provo businesses track the wrong Google Ads metrics, focusing on vanity statistics rather than business-impact measurements. Clicks, impressions, and click-through rates provide minimal insight into campaign profitability and growth contribution.

**Primary Performance Indicators:**
1. **Cost-per-Acquisition (CPA):** Total advertising spend divided by qualified customers acquired
2. **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS):** Revenue generated per dollar spent on advertising  
3. **Customer Lifetime Value to CPA Ratio:** Long-term customer value compared to acquisition cost
4. **Qualified Lead Conversion Rate:** Percentage of clicks that become sales-qualified opportunities

**Secondary Optimization Metrics:**
- Search impression share for target keywords
- Quality Score trends across ad groups  
- Geographic performance variations
- Time-of-day and day-of-week conversion patterns
