## How to Measure Online Customer Acquisition Success

Effective measurement focuses on revenue-generating metrics rather than vanity statistics. Bend businesses should track customer lifetime value, cost per acquisition, and return on ad spend rather than impressions, clicks, or social media followers.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Total marketing spend divided by new customers acquired. Bend service businesses should target CAC below 20% of customer lifetime value.

**Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: Revenue generated divided by advertising investment. Profitable campaigns typically achieve 3:1 ROAS minimum, with successful campaigns reaching 5:1 or higher.

**Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Average revenue per customer over their entire relationship. Bend's community-focused market supports higher CLV through repeat business and referrals.

**Lead Quality Score**: Percentage of leads that convert to paying customers. High-quality campaigns deliver 15-25% conversion rates for service businesses.

### Tracking Tools and Implementation

Google Analytics 4 provides comprehensive tracking for website conversions and customer journeys. Call tracking software measures phone conversions from online campaigns. CRM integration connects marketing activities to actual revenue generated.

Proper tracking requires:
- Conversion tracking on all lead forms and phone calls
- UTM parameters identifying traffic sources
- CRM integration connecting leads to revenue
- Monthly reporting focused on customer acquisition metrics
