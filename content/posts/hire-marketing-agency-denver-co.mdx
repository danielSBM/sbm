## How Much Does a Marketing Agency Cost in Denver?

Marketing agency pricing in Denver follows three primary models, with costs varying significantly based on approach and results guarantees. Traditional full-service agencies charge between $8,000-$25,000 monthly retainers, while specialized digital marketing firms typically range from $3,500-$12,000 per month, and emerging pay-per-result models charge only when delivering qualified customers.

### Traditional Retainer Agency Costs

Denver's established advertising agencies—concentrated in downtown's Central Business District and the Denver Tech Center—typically structure pricing around monthly retainers:

- **Large Full-Service Agencies**: $15,000-$50,000/month
- **Mid-Size Digital Agencies**: $5,000-$20,000/month  
- **Boutique Specialists**: $3,000-$12,000/month
- **Freelancer Collectives**: $2,000-$8,000/month

These retainer fees represent fixed monthly costs regardless of performance. A Denver manufacturing company might pay $18,000 monthly for comprehensive services but receive leads costing $800 each in months with poor performance, while a strong month might deliver leads at $200 each—creating unpredictable cost-per-acquisition metrics.

### Pay-Per-Result Alternative

Pay-per-result marketing agencies charge only when delivering measurable outcomes like qualified leads, new customers, or revenue targets. This model eliminates the risk of paying for marketing activities that don't generate business growth.

For Denver businesses, pay-per-result typically costs 15-30% less annually than equivalent retainer relationships while providing guaranteed performance metrics. A B2B services company in Denver might pay $400-800 per qualified lead through pay-per-result versus $200-1,200 per lead through retainer agencies, but with guaranteed lead quality and conversion tracking.
