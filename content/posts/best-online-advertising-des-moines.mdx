## Measuring success: KPIs that matter for Des Moines businesses

Customer acquisition cost (CAC) and lifetime value (LTV) ratios determine advertising success for Des Moines small businesses, with healthy campaigns maintaining 3:1 to 5:1 LTV:CAC ratios. Unlike vanity metrics such as impressions or social followers, these revenue-focused measurements directly correlate with business growth and profitability.

### Revenue-Based Performance Metrics

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**
Total advertising spend divided by customers acquired. Des Moines businesses should track CAC by channel, campaign, and geographic area to optimize budget allocation.

*Industry CAC Benchmarks for Des Moines:*
- Home services: $85-$250 per customer
- Professional services: $120-$400 per client
- Retail: $25-$85 per customer
- Healthcare: $90-$300 per patient
- B2B services: $200-$800 per client

**Lifetime Value (LTV):**
Total revenue generated from average customer relationship. Des Moines businesses often underestimate LTV by focusing only on initial purchase value rather than repeat business and referrals.

*Calculating Accurate LTV:*
- Track customer retention rates monthly
- Include upsell and cross-sell revenue
- Factor in referral generation from satisfied customers
- Account for seasonal variations in Des Moines market

**Return on Ad Spend (ROAS):**
Revenue generated divided by advertising investment. Healthy ROAS varies by industry but should exceed 3:1 for most Des Moines small businesses to maintain profitability after operational costs.
