## Measuring Success: KPIs That Matter for Des Moines Businesses

Success measurement for Des Moines customer acquisition should focus on revenue-generating metrics rather than vanity statistics. The most important KPIs directly correlate with business growth and profitability.

### Primary Revenue Metrics

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Total marketing spend divided by new customers acquired. Des Moines businesses should track CAC by channel to identify the most cost-effective customer sources.

**Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Average revenue per customer over their entire relationship with your business. CLV should exceed CAC by at least 3:1 for sustainable growth.

**Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: Revenue generated divided by advertising costs. Profitable campaigns typically achieve 4:1 ROAS or higher.

**Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: For subscription or service businesses, MRR indicates predictable revenue growth from marketing efforts.

### Secondary Performance Indicators

**Conversion Rate by Channel**: Percentage of visitors who become customers from each marketing source. This metric reveals which channels attract the highest-quality prospects.

**Average Order Value**: Revenue per transaction, useful for optimizing pricing and upselling strategies.

**Lead-to-Customer Conversion Rate**: Percentage of leads who become paying customers, indicating lead quality and sales process effectiveness.

**Time to Conversion**: Average time from first contact to customer conversion, helping optimize nurturing sequences and sales cycles.

### Tracking Implementation

Des Moines businesses need proper tracking infrastructure to measure these KPIs accurately:

**Google Analytics 4**: Configure conversion tracking for phone calls, form submissions, and online purchases

**CRM Integration**: Connect marketing platforms to customer databases for complete attribution tracking

**Call Tracking**: Use unique phone numbers for different marketing channels to attribute phone-based conversions

**UTM Parameters**: Tag all marketing links with source, medium, and campaign parameters for accurate traffic attribution

Without proper tracking, businesses cannot optimize marketing spend or identify the most profitable customer acquisition channels.
