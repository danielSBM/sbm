## Measuring Marketing Success in Fort Worth

Successful Fort Worth businesses track an average of 12-15 key performance indicators across their marketing efforts, with customer acquisition cost, lifetime value, and attribution accuracy serving as primary success metrics in the competitive Dallas-Fort Worth marketplace. However, 61% of businesses struggle with accurate measurement due to inadequate tracking infrastructure and unclear attribution models.

### Essential Metrics for Fort Worth Businesses

**Primary Performance Indicators:**
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): Total marketing spend ÷ new customers acquired
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): Average revenue per customer over entire relationship
- CAC to CLV Ratio: Should be 1:3 or better for sustainable growth
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): Revenue generated ÷ advertising investment
- Marketing Qualified Leads (MQL): Leads meeting predetermined quality criteria

**Channel-Specific Metrics:**
- Organic search: Rankings, traffic, conversion rates
- Paid search: Click-through rates, quality scores, conversion costs
- Social media: Engagement rates, follower growth, social conversions
- Email marketing: Open rates, click rates, list growth
- Content marketing: Time on page, social shares, lead generation
