## Measuring Facebook Ad Success: Key Metrics for Santa Fe Businesses

Effective Facebook advertising measurement requires tracking metrics aligned with your Santa Fe business objectives rather than focusing solely on vanity metrics like reach or impressions. Successful campaigns optimize for customer acquisition cost (CAC) and lifetime value (LTV) rather than clicks or engagement.

### Primary Performance Metrics

**Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**
Calculate total revenue generated divided by total advertising costs. Santa Fe businesses should target minimum 3:1 ROAS, with top performers achieving 5-8:1 during peak seasons.

**Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)**  
Track the total cost to acquire each new customer through Facebook advertising. Compare CPA across different campaigns, audiences, and creative variations to optimize budget allocation.

**Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**
Measure the total revenue generated from customers acquired through Facebook ads over 6-12 months. Santa Fe businesses with strong retention see LTV:CPA ratios of 8:1 or higher.

### Secondary Optimization Metrics

Monitor these metrics for campaign optimization insights:
- **Click-through rate (CTR)**: Indicates creative and targeting effectiveness
- **Cost per click (CPC)**: Measures auction competitiveness and relevance
- **Conversion rate**: Percentage of clicks resulting in desired actions
- **Frequency**: Average number of times users see your ads
- **Relevance score**: Facebook's assessment of ad quality and audience match

### Attribution and Tracking Setup

Implement comprehensive tracking systems to measure Facebook advertising impact accurately:

**Facebook Pixel Configuration**
Install Facebook Pixel on all website pages and configure custom events for key actions (purchases, form submissions, phone calls). Use Google Tag Manager for easier implementation and management.

**Google Analytics Integration**
