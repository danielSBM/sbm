## How Do Albuquerque Businesses Measure Online Marketing Success?

Successful Albuquerque businesses track customer acquisition cost (CAC), lifetime value (LTV), and return on ad spend (ROAS) rather than vanity metrics like website visitors or social media followers. ### Essential Metrics for ROI Tracking

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** measures the total cost to acquire each new customer through digital marketing. Albuquerque businesses should calculate CAC by channel to identify the most efficient customer sources.

**Lifetime Value (LTV)** represents the total revenue generated by average customers over their relationship with the business. Service-based businesses in Albuquerque typically see LTV ranging from $800-$3,500 depending on industry and service frequency.

**Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)** shows revenue generated per dollar invested in advertising. Profitable campaigns typically achieve 3:1 ROAS minimum, with exceptional performers reaching 8:1 or higher.
