## How do St. George marketing agencies structure their pricing?

Most marketing agencies in St. George use monthly retainer models that prioritize predictable agency revenue over client results. Understanding these structures helps business owners identify red flags before signing contracts.

**Monthly Retainer Model (85% of St. George agencies):**
Agencies charge fixed monthly fees ranging from $2,500-$12,000 regardless of performance. Retainers typically include 20-40 hours of work monthly, with additional hours billed at $125-$200 rates. This model protects agencies from performance risk while leaving clients vulnerable to months of poor results.

**Project-Based Pricing (10% of agencies):**
Some St. George firms offer project pricing for specific deliverables like website redesigns or SEO audits. Projects range from $1,500-$25,000 depending on scope. While project pricing provides cost certainty, it often lacks ongoing optimization crucial for sustained results.

**Percentage of Ad Spend (5% of agencies):**
A small subset charges 15-25% of total advertising spend as management fees. For businesses spending $10,000 monthly on Google Ads, this adds $1,500-$2,500 in management costs. This model aligns agency incentives with spending more money, not necessarily generating better results.

**Hybrid Pricing Models:**
Some agencies combine retainers with performance bonuses or ad spend percentages. While seemingly balanced, these models often include minimum spending requirements that benefit agencies more than St. George businesses.

The fundamental problem with all traditional models: agencies get paid whether they deliver qualified leads, sales, or any measurable business impact.

## What questions should you ask St. George marketing agencies?

Before committing to any marketing agreement in St. George, ask specific questions designed to identify agencies focused on results versus those optimizing for their own revenue collection.

**Essential Performance Questions:**

1. **"What specific, measurable results will you deliver in 90 days?"**
   Avoid agencies offering vague promises like "increased brand awareness" or "improved online presence." Demand concrete metrics: number of qualified leads, cost per acquisition targets, or revenue attribution goals.

   Results-focused agencies will describe optimization processes, additional effort allocation, or performance guarantees. Agencies focused on retainer collection will cite "market conditions" or "long-term strategy" without accountability measures.

   St. George businesses often serve customers from Las Vegas, Phoenix, and Salt Lake City markets. Agencies should demonstrate multi-market expertise rather than purely local focus.
