- Transparent attribution and ROI tracking

## Measuring Success: Key Metrics for Washington Small Business Advertising

Effective advertising measurement requires tracking metrics that directly correlate with business growth rather than vanity metrics that look impressive but don't drive revenue.

### Primary Performance Indicators

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Total advertising spend divided by new customers acquired. Washington businesses should target CAC at 20-30% of customer lifetime value, though professional services can justify higher ratios.

**Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: Revenue attributed to advertising divided by advertising cost. Sustainable Washington businesses typically achieve 3:1 to 5:1 ROAS, with professional services often seeing higher ratios due to customer lifetime value.

**Lead Quality Score**: Percentage of leads that meet qualification criteria and advance through sales processes. High-quality campaigns generate 60%+ qualified leads rather than focusing purely on volume.

### Attribution and Tracking Setup

Washington businesses must implement proper tracking to accurately measure advertising performance across multiple touchpoints and longer sales cycles common in professional services.

**Google Analytics 4 Setup**: Configure enhanced ecommerce tracking and custom conversions aligned with business objectives (phone calls, form submissions, appointment bookings).

**Call Tracking**: Use dynamic number insertion for businesses where phone calls represent primary conversion actions. Essential for medical practices, legal services, and home improvement companies.

**CRM Integration**: Connect advertising platforms with customer relationship management systems to track complete customer journeys from initial click to final purchase and beyond.

### Competitive Benchmarking

Regular competitive analysis helps Washington businesses maintain market position and identify opportunities for campaign optimization.

**Auction Insights**: Google Ads auction insight reports reveal competitor overlap, average position, and impression share data for strategic adjustments.

**Social Media Monitoring**: Track competitor social media advertising frequency, creative approaches, and engagement rates to identify market gaps.
