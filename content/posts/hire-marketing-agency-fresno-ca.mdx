## How to Evaluate Marketing Agencies in Fresno?

Evaluating marketing agencies in Fresno requires moving beyond impressive presentations to examine actual performance data and alignment with your business model. The most critical evaluation criteria focus on measurable results, industry expertise, and pricing transparency.

**Performance Verification Process:**

1. **Request Case Studies with Data:** Demand specific metrics from similar Fresno or Central Valley businesses, including cost-per-lead, conversion rates, and revenue attribution data.

2. **Analyze Their Own Marketing:** Agencies that can't generate leads for themselves rarely succeed for clients. Examine their Google Ads, social media presence, and search rankings.

3. **Reference Check Methodology:** Contact current and former clients directly. Ask specific questions about results delivered, communication quality, and contract satisfaction.

4. **Technical Audit Capabilities:** Request a sample technical audit of your current marketing. Quality agencies provide detailed, actionable insights even before being hired.

**Red Flags to Avoid:**

- Agencies requiring 12+ month contracts without performance guarantees
- Firms that don't provide transparent reporting or data access
- Companies using generic case studies from outside the Central Valley region
- Agencies that cannot explain their attribution methodology
- Firms charging setup fees exceeding $5,000 for standard implementations
