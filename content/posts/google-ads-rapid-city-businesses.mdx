## Measuring Google Ads Success: KPIs That Matter for Rapid City Businesses

**Rapid City businesses should focus on 4 primary KPIs: cost per qualified lead, lead-to-customer conversion rate, customer lifetime value, and local market share growth.** Vanity metrics like impressions and clicks don't correlate with business growth.

### Primary Performance Metrics

**Cost Per Qualified Lead (CPQL):**
The total advertising spend divided by leads meeting your qualification criteria. For Rapid City markets, qualified leads typically include:
- Geographic qualification (within service area)  
- Need qualification (actual interest in your service)
- Budget qualification (ability to afford your solution)
- Timeline qualification (ready to purchase within reasonable timeframe)

**Lead-to-Customer Conversion Rate:**
Percentage of qualified leads that become paying customers. This metric reveals sales process effectiveness separate from advertising performance. Rapid City businesses typically achieve:
- Professional services: 15-35% conversion rates
- Home services: 25-45% conversion rates  
- Healthcare: 35-55% conversion rates
- Retail/e-commerce: 2-8% conversion rates

**Customer Lifetime Value (CLV):**
Total revenue generated per customer over their entire relationship with your business. CLV determines sustainable acquisition costs and campaign profitability.

**Local Market Share Growth:**  
Your percentage of total search volume and conversions in Rapid City for relevant keywords. Tools like SEMrush provide competitive market share estimates.

### Secondary Performance Metrics

**Quality Score Trends:** Higher quality scores reduce costs and improve ad positions
**Impression Share:** Percentage of available impressions your ads receive
**Geographic Performance:** Cost and conversion variations across service areas  
**Time-to-Conversion:** Average days from initial click to customer conversion

### Reporting Frequency and Analysis

**Weekly Reports:**
- Lead volume and cost trends

- Quality score and impression share changes

**Monthly Reports:**  
- Customer conversion rates and lifetime value analysis
