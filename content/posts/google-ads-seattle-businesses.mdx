## Measuring Google Ads Success for Seattle Businesses

Google Ads success measurement requires tracking qualified leads and customer acquisition costs rather than vanity metrics like impressions and clicks, with Seattle businesses needing conversion rates above 3% and cost-per-acquisition below 20% of customer lifetime value to achieve sustainable profitability. ### Essential KPIs for Seattle Google Ads Campaigns

**Lead Quality Metrics:**
- **Qualified Lead Rate**: Percentage of form submissions or calls that meet ideal customer criteria
- **Lead-to-Customer Conversion**: How many qualified leads become paying customers
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: Total revenue generated per Google Ads-acquired customer
- **Payback Period**: Time required for customer revenue to exceed acquisition costs

**Campaign Performance Metrics:**
- **Cost Per Qualified Lead**: Total spend divided by qualified leads (not total leads)
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: Revenue generated per dollar spent on advertising
- **Quality Score Trends**: Keyword-level quality indicators affecting costs and ad position
- **Impression Share**: Percentage of available impressions captured in target markets

### Attribution and Tracking Setup

**Multi-Touch Attribution Implementation:**
Seattle businesses with longer sales cycles (B2B services, high-value purchases) require attribution models beyond last-click to understand Google Ads' full impact. First-click attribution reveals initial awareness generation, while time-decay models credit touchpoints closer to conversion more heavily.

**Offline Conversion Tracking:**
Service businesses conducting sales via phone or in-person meetings must implement offline conversion import to measure Google Ads effectiveness accurately. This process connects CRM data back to original ad clicks, providing complete customer journey visibility.

**Call Tracking Integration:**
Phone-dependent businesses like healthcare practices, legal services, and home repair companies need call tracking numbers on landing pages and Google Ads call extensions. This enables measurement of phone conversion rates and conversation quality analysis.
