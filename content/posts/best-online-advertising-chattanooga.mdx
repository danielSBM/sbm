**Traditional Agency Model:**
- Monthly retainer: $4,500-$8,500

- Monthly retainer: $0

Pay-per-result models transfer performance risk to the advertising provider. This alignment ensures that advertising efforts focus exclusively on activities that generate measurable customer acquisition rather than vanity metrics like impressions or clicks.

## Measuring online advertising success in Chattanooga

Online advertising success for Chattanooga businesses requires tracking customer acquisition cost, lifetime customer value, and return on advertising spend rather than vanity metrics like impressions or website traffic. Effective measurement focuses on qualified customer delivery and revenue attribution within 30-90 day windows to account for typical sales cycles in mid-size markets.

### Key performance indicators (KPIs)

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Total advertising spend divided by qualified customers acquired. Chattanooga businesses typically see CAC ranging from $85-$450 depending on industry and average deal value.

**Lifetime Customer Value (LCV):** Total revenue generated per customer over the relationship duration. Service businesses in Chattanooga report average LCV of $2,500-$15,000, creating strong ROI potential when CAC remains below 20% of LCV.

**Return on Advertising Spend (ROAS):** Revenue generated divided by advertising costs. Profitable campaigns maintain ROAS of 3:1 or higher, meaning $3 in revenue for every $1 in advertising spend.

**Conversion Rate:** Percentage of advertising clicks that result in qualified customer inquiries. Chattanooga businesses average 4.8-12.6% conversion rates across different advertising channels.
