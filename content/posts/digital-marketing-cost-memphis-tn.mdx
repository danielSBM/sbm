## How to evaluate digital marketing ROI in Memphis market conditions

Measuring digital marketing ROI requires tracking specific metrics aligned with Memphis market conditions and local business objectives. Successful ROI evaluation goes beyond vanity metrics to focus on revenue generation, customer acquisition costs, and lifetime value optimization.

### Essential Memphis Marketing Metrics

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Total marketing spend divided by new customers acquired. Memphis businesses should target CAC ratios of 3:1 to 5:1 (customer lifetime value to acquisition cost).

**Local Search Visibility:** Track rankings for Memphis-specific keywords and "near me" searches. Local visibility directly correlates with foot traffic and service inquiries for Memphis businesses.

**Lead Quality Score:** Measure lead qualification rates by source. Memphis B2B companies should achieve 15-25% qualification rates from digital campaigns, while consumer services target 8-15% rates.

**Revenue Attribution:** Connect marketing touchpoints to actual sales revenue. Memphis businesses need clear attribution models showing which campaigns generate paying customers versus mere website traffic.
