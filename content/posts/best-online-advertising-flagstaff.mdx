## Measuring Online Advertising Success in Flagstaff

Successful advertising measurement requires tracking metrics that directly correlate with business growth rather than vanity statistics. Flagstaff businesses should focus on customer acquisition cost, lifetime value, and return on advertising spend rather than impressions or click-through rates.

### Key Performance Indicators for Small Business

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) serves as the primary metric for evaluating advertising effectiveness—successful Flagstaff businesses maintain CAC below 20% of average customer lifetime value.** Tourism businesses typically accept higher CAC during peak seasons due to increased transaction values.

Essential KPIs include:

**Primary Metrics**:
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): Total advertising spend ÷ new customers acquired
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): Revenue generated ÷ advertising investment
- Cost Per Lead (CPL): Advertising spend ÷ qualified leads generated
- Conversion Rate: Percentage of clicks/visits that become customers
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): Average revenue per customer over relationship duration

**Secondary Metrics**:
- Click-through rates for optimization insights
- Geographic performance by targeting zone
- Seasonal performance variations
- Device performance (mobile vs. desktop)
- Channel performance comparisons
