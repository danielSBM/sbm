### Essential Tracking Metrics

**Revenue Metrics:**
- Cost Per Acquisition (CPA): Total ad spend ÷ number of customers acquired
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): Average customer value over entire relationship
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): Revenue generated ÷ ad spend
- Revenue Attribution: Connecting specific campaigns to closed sales

**Operational Metrics:**
- Lead Quality Score: Percentage of leads that become customers
- Time to Conversion: Days from first click to customer acquisition
- Geographic Performance: ROI by Dallas neighborhoods and suburbs
- Campaign Performance: Individual ad group and keyword analysis

**Competitive Metrics:**
- Market Share: Percentage of available search traffic captured
- Cost Efficiency: CPA compared to industry benchmarks
- Quality Score: Google's assessment of ad relevance and landing page quality
- Impression Share: Percentage of available impressions captured
