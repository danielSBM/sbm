## How Do You Measure Online Advertising Success in Dallas Markets?

Customer acquisition cost (CAC) provides the most important metric for Dallas small businesses evaluating advertising performance. Calculate total advertising spend divided by new customers acquired to determine per-customer investment and compare against customer lifetime value projections.

Return on ad spend (ROAS) measures revenue generated per advertising dollar invested. Dallas service businesses should target minimum 3:1 ROAS, meaning every $1,000 in advertising generates $3,000+ in customer revenue within 90-180 days depending on sales cycle length.

### Essential Tracking Metrics for Dallas Businesses

**Primary Performance Indicators**:
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): Total ad spend ÷ new customers
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): Customer revenue ÷ ad spend  
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): Average customer total revenue
- CAC Payback Period: Months to recover customer acquisition cost

**Secondary Optimization Metrics**:
- Cost-per-click by platform and campaign
- Conversion rate by traffic source and landing page
- Geographic performance within Dallas metro area
- Mobile vs desktop performance ratios

### Setting Realistic Performance Goals

New campaigns require 60-90 days for algorithm optimization and audience refinement. Dallas businesses should establish baseline metrics during initial months, then set improvement targets based on industry benchmarks and business objectives.

**Goal-Setting Framework**:
