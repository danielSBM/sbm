## Measuring Facebook Ad Success: KPIs That Matter for Salt Lake City Businesses

Salt Lake City businesses should focus on revenue-impact metrics rather than engagement vanity metrics when evaluating Facebook advertising performance. Customer acquisition cost (CAC) and lifetime value (LTV) ratios provide the clearest indication of campaign profitability and sustainable growth potential.

**Primary Performance Indicators:**

1. **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)**: Total ad spend divided by qualified customers acquired
2. **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: Revenue generated divided by advertising investment
3. **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Total revenue per customer over relationship duration
4. **LTV:CAC Ratio**: Lifetime value divided by acquisition cost (target: 3:1 minimum)

**Secondary Optimization Metrics:**

- **Cost Per Click (CPC)**: Indicates audience relevance and creative effectiveness
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: Measures ad appeal and targeting accuracy
- **Conversion Rate**: Reveals landing page and offer alignment
- **Frequency**: Prevents audience fatigue and creative burnout
