Return on investment calculation for Flagstaff marketing campaigns requires tracking customer lifetime value against acquisition costs, with successful campaigns generating $3-$5 revenue for every $1 invested. Tourism businesses should calculate ROI differently during peak versus off-seasons due to dramatic booking value fluctuations.

**Essential ROI Metrics for Flagstaff Businesses:**

**Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) by Industry:**
- Hotels/lodging: $280-$850 (depending on stay duration and repeat visits)
- Restaurants: $85-$320 (based on average check and visit frequency)
- Retail/tourist shops: $45-$180 (varying by product mix and seasonality)

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Targets:**
Tourism businesses should target CAC at 20-25% of CLV during peak seasons, 15-18% during shoulder seasons. This accounts for higher competition and advertising costs during busy periods.
