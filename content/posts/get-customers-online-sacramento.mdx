### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Successful Sacramento businesses track specific metrics that directly correlate with revenue growth and customer acquisition efficiency. These KPIs provide actionable insights for marketing optimization and budget allocation decisions.

**Essential Sacramento Business KPIs:**
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): Total marketing spend ÷ new customers acquired
- Customer Lifetime Value (LTV): Average purchase value × purchase frequency × customer lifespan
- LTV:CAC Ratio: Target minimum 3:1, optimal 5:1 or higher
- Lead-to-customer conversion rate: Qualified leads that become paying customers
- Channel attribution: Which marketing channels drive highest-value customers

Sacramento's diverse economy requires industry-specific benchmarking. Professional services typically achieve 6:1 LTV:CAC ratios, while retail businesses average 4:1 ratios due to lower margins and higher competition.
