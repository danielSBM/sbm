## How to Evaluate Marketing Agency Results in Topeka?

Marketing agency performance evaluation requires tracking specific metrics that directly correlate with business revenue growth rather than vanity metrics like website traffic or social media engagement. Topeka businesses should demand monthly reporting on customer acquisition costs, qualified lead generation, and return on advertising spend with clear attribution to agency efforts.

### Key Performance Indicators for Agency Success

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Calculate the total marketing investment required to acquire each new customer, including agency fees and advertising spend. Effective agencies consistently reduce CAC over time through optimization and improved targeting.

**Qualified Lead Generation:** Track leads meeting specific criteria such as budget capacity, decision-making authority, and genuine purchase intent. Raw lead volume means nothing without quality qualification that indicates likely conversion to paying customers.

**Return on Advertising Spend (ROAS):** Measure revenue generated per dollar invested in paid advertising campaigns. Successful campaigns typically achieve 3:1 to 6:1 ROAS depending on industry and customer lifetime value.

### Monthly Reporting Requirements

Demand comprehensive monthly reports including campaign performance data, competitive analysis updates, and strategic recommendations for improvement. Reports should clearly connect marketing activities to business outcomes rather than focusing on activity metrics.

Essential monthly reporting elements:
- Revenue attribution by marketing channel and campaign
- Cost per acquisition trends and optimization opportunities
- Competitive landscape changes affecting campaign performance
- Strategic recommendations based on performance data analysis

### Quarterly Business Reviews

Schedule quarterly reviews focusing on overall business impact rather than monthly campaign fluctuations. These sessions should evaluate marketing strategy effectiveness, discuss market changes affecting performance, and plan strategic adjustments for upcoming quarters.

Quarterly reviews should address:
- Overall ROI analysis and business growth correlation
- Market trend impacts on customer acquisition strategies
- Competitive positioning changes and response strategies
- Strategic planning for seasonal fluctuations and opportunities
